FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with Insights AI? +

Simply sign up for a free account, verify your email, and you'll be taken to your dashboard where you can start using our AI chatbot and analytics tools immediately.

Is there a free trial available? +

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

What types of data can I analyze? +

You can analyze text data including customer feedback, reviews, social media posts, surveys, support tickets, and more. We support various file formats including CSV, Excel, and JSON.

How accurate is the AI analysis? +

Our AI models achieve 95%+ accuracy in sentiment analysis and text classification. We continuously improve our models with new data and feedback.

Can I integrate with my existing tools? +

Absolutely! We offer 500+ integrations including popular tools like Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, and more. We also provide a comprehensive REST API for custom integrations.

Is my data secure? +

Yes, we take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, we're SOC 2 certified, and GDPR compliant. Your data is never shared with third parties.
